Great Weapon Type: Great Sword
Reach: 5ft
Hit Bonus: +6
Damage: 1d8+5

Arcing Strike: 
 This weapon is built around a chain that the weilder can extend and restrict at will. This increases the range of attacks as well as the number of targets that can be hit one swing
- While the chain is extended this weapons reach is doubled
- Wide Swing - Attack three targets within range in one swing, dealing 1d6+5 to each target
